TradeLab Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 20, 2026

中文
Important: We take your privacy seriously. TradeLab is a K-line training app that follows the principle of minimal data collection and does not collect any personal identification information.

1. Data Collection

We only collect the following necessary data to provide app functionality:

  • Anonymous User ID: A locally generated random identifier (e.g., TL_a1b2c3d4) to distinguish different users
  • App Preferences: Theme preferences, indicator configurations, training settings, etc.
  • Training Statistics: Number of sessions, win rate, holding time, etc. (stored locally only)

We do NOT collect:

  • Name, email, phone number, or other personal identification information
  • Precise geolocation
  • Device advertising identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • Photos, videos, or other media files

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device (using MMKV secure storage) and is not uploaded to cloud servers. You can at any time:

  • Export Data: Tap "Export My Data" in the "Me" page
  • Delete Data: Tap "Delete Account and Data" in the "Me" page

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. Your data is stored only on your device.

4. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: View all data we collect
  • Right to Data Portability: Export your data in JSON format
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of all your data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw privacy consent at any time

5. Children's Privacy

TradeLab is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be notified in the app and the "Last Updated" date on this page will be updated.
